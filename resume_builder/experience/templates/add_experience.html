Writing Tips for Strong Experiences

Use the STAR Method

Situation: Set the context
Task: Describe your responsibility
Action: Explain what you did
Result: Share the outcome

Quantify Results

Include specific numbers, percentages, timeframes, or metrics wherever possible. "Increased sales by 25%" is stronger than "Improved sales performance".

Focus on Impact

Emphasize the value you created, problems you solved, and positive changes you drove. What was different because of your contribution?
